About Us

Serving the wonderful community of Bugho, Samar, Barrio Kopi is your go-to destination for everyday value. Though we opened our doors in 2024, our mission is to become a long-lasting, dependable fixture of the neighborhood.

We pride ourselves on offering an extensive variety of affordable drinks and satisfying snacks, making quality coffee accessible to everyone. Step inside and let the fresh, modern design blend with a tranquil nature ambiance and relaxing music, offering a perfect, unplugged sanctuary for every guest.
